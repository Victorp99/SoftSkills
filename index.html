Soft Skills

Soft skills can be described as the personal character traits or qualities each of us has. These skills can not be learned by enrolling in a training program, but through experiences that help you develop these skills. Soft skills give us the ability to effectively and efficiently use our techincal skills and knowledge. IT organizations are now looking for candidates who have both soft and hard skills. A lot of candidates tend to lack in soft skills. Below are examples of soft skills.
